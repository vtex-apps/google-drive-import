## About

This app allows SKU images to be bulk imported into the VTEX Catalog from a user's Google Drive.

## Features

- Bulk import SKU images into the VTEX Catalog from a Google Drive folder
- Automatically import images as soon as they are uploaded
- Imported images are moved to "Done" and "Error" folders
- Use the same Google Drive account to import images into multiple VTEX store accounts

## Configuration

1. Install this app from the VTEX app store.

2. In your account's admin dashboard, go to `Catalog > Google Drive Import`.

3. Follow the instructions provided on the admin page to grant access to your Google Drive and upload your images with the proper naming convention.
